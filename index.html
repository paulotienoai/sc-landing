Frequently Asked Questions

Scalp Micropigmentation is a non-surgical cosmetic procedure that uses specialized pigmentation techniques to replicate the natural appearance of hair follicles on your scalp. It creates the illusion of a fuller head of hair or a defined hairline without surgery.

No, SMP is NOT a hair transplant. Hair transplants involve surgically moving hair follicles from one area to another. SMP is a cosmetic tattoo that creates the appearance of hair follicles through pigmentation - no surgery required.

SMP typically lasts 4-6 years before requiring a touch-up. The longevity depends on factors like skin type, sun exposure, and aftercare. Touch-ups are simple and help maintain the fresh appearance.

Most clients report minimal discomfort during the procedure. We use topical numbing agents to ensure your comfort. The sensation is often described as less painful than a regular tattoo.

SMP typically ranges from $1,500 to $4,500 depending on the extent of treatment needed. During your consultation, we'll provide a detailed quote based on your specific situation and goals.

SMP works for most people experiencing hair loss, including those with receding hairlines, thinning hair, pattern baldness, alopecia, or scalp scars. It's suitable for all skin types and hair colors. We'll assess your specific situation during the consultation.
